# Stakeholder-Review — Kaffeelisten Phase 2

A standalone, self-contained HTML document to send to the people involved in the
ITC1 rollout — your teammate and the two CEOs — to confirm the plan and gather
their preferences before the next phase is built.

## File

- [`kaffeelisten-phase-2-feedback.html`](kaffeelisten-phase-2-feedback.html) —
  open it in any browser. No internet, fonts or build step required (everything
  is inlined), so it works as an email attachment or a printout.

## How to use it

1. Open the file once yourself and adjust the **reply-to address** near the top
   of the `<script>` block (`REPLY_TO`) if feedback should go somewhere other
   than `hlexhelftd@gmail.com`.
2. Send it. Two good options:
   - **Email attachment** — recipients open it in their browser, fill it in, and
     click *"Antworten per E-Mail senden"*. Their answers are composed into a
     pre-filled email back to you.
   - **PDF** — open it and use *"Als PDF drucken / speichern"* (or the browser's
     print dialog) if someone prefers to annotate on paper.

The document is read-and-reply: the form fields are a convenience for collecting
answers, nothing is sent or stored anywhere until the recipient clicks the
email button.

## Audience note

It is written in German (CEO-facing). The technical "fehlt etwas?" questions are
relevant to your teammate; the feature/feel questions are aimed at the CEOs and
day-to-day users. Everyone can answer the parts that apply to them.
