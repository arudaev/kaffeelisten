- **3.80-8.00s** - beat opens with kick, clap, hats; product panels cascade.
- **8.00-12.20s** - bass line becomes rounder; confirmation path and totals land on beats.
- **12.20-16.50s** - light lift; dashboard metrics count in and report path travels.
- **16.50-20.00s** - warm resolve; logo lockup, URL, and final tagline hold.
