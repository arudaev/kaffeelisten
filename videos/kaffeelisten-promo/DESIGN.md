# Design System

## Overview

Kaffeelisten is a warm, minimal PWA with a deliberately quiet interface. The landing page uses a centered one-line coffee illustration, a large Inter wordmark, and a single amber primary action on a stone background. The product screenshots show the same restrained system extended into large iPad-friendly selection tiles, item cards, confirmation rows, and an admin dashboard. The video should feel fast, practical, and campus-grounded rather than glossy or decorative.

## Colors

- **Page Stone**: `#FAFAF9` - primary background from the live site.
- **Surface White**: `#FFFFFF` - cards, dashboard panels, device surfaces.
- **Text Primary**: `#1C1917` - black-brown primary text and icon strokes.
- **Border Quiet**: `#E5E7EB` - thin product borders and dividers from the capture.
- **Brand Amber**: `#D97706` - primary CTA, selected states, progress accents.
- **Text Muted**: `#A8A29E` - footer labels and secondary metadata.
- **Amber Hover**: `#B45309` - darker emphasis for transitions and strokes.
- **Amber Wash**: `#FFFBEB` - selected tile backgrounds from the design system.
- **Success Green**: `#16A34A` - admin active status accents.

## Typography

- **Primary**: Inter (400, 500, 600, 700). This is the captured site font and must remain the product voice.
- **Display use**: Inter 700/800 at 76-124px for short claims, tight line-height, no negative tracking.
- **Interface use**: Inter 500/600 at 20-36px for German UI labels and product callouts.
- **Data use**: Inter 600/700 with tabular numerals for prices, monthly totals, and counters.

## Elevation

Depth comes from real product cards: white surfaces over stone backgrounds, 1px quiet borders, 8-16px radii, and light soft shadows. The promo can exaggerate this slightly with stacked screenshots, perspective tilt, and amber glows, but it should not introduce glassmorphism, neon, or heavy cinematic darkness.

## Components

- **One-Line Coffee Mark**: black stroke cup with steam, used as the opening and closing brand anchor.
- **Primary Amber CTA**: compact rounded rectangle, white bold text, practical and direct.
- **Member Flow Tiles**: large white list rows and item cards with amber selected borders and subtle shadows.
- **Step Progress Dots**: tiny amber and stone dots that communicate the fast flow.
- **Confirmation Receipt**: table-like white card with person, company, item rows, and total.
- **Admin Metrics Strip**: white stat cards with amber left borders and bold numbers.
- **Admin Data Table**: dense operational rows with quiet borders and tabular values.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's

- Use `#FAFAF9`, `#FFFFFF`, `#1C1917`, and `#D97706` as the dominant palette.
- Keep German UI copy visible in screenshots and on-screen labels.
- Use product screenshots generously and animate them with pans, tilts, and stacked motion.
- Make all numbers and prices readable with tabular numerals.
- Let the amber accent guide the viewer's eye from tap flow to report action.

### Don'ts

- Do not add login, payment, invoice, or multi-location language.
- Do not use neon, dark tech gradients, glass panels, or generic SaaS purple.
- Do not replace the product font with a decorative font.
- Do not turn the promo into a landing page explainer; keep it product-first and kinetic.
- Do not use emoji or lorem ipsum in visible UI copy.
